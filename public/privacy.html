Information We Collect

TypeMaster collects information to provide better services to our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Typing performance data (speed, accuracy, practice sessions)
  • Account information (email, username, preferences)
  • Usage analytics (pages visited, features used)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our typing practice services
  • Track your progress and provide personalized recommendations
  • Communicate with you about updates and new features
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly create an account. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.
